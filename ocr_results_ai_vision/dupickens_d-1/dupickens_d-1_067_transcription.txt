Henry Vance
to
Daniel Kephart or Hephart

State of Indiana
Jackson County

This indenture witnesseth that Henry
Vance of the state and County aforesaid for and
in consideration of the love and affection I bear to my beloved son
in law Richard Hephart have given and confirmed unto the said
your acres hereunto mentioned and by these presents give bargain sell and transfer all
my labors and profits before marriage and give also bargain transfer and sell all the
increase that are the lawful begotten issue of my person or body and that is with her
before or after. Richard Hephart have and to hold forever. In witness whereof I
have hereunto set my hand and seal this 20th day of April in the year of our Lord one
thousand eight hundred and thirty eight. Signed sealed and delivered in presence of
us. Witness my hand and seal this Twenty fifth day of April in the year one thousand
eight hundred and thirty eight. 
James Herring
John H. Thompson

Signed and sealed in presence of us James H. Thompson
State of Indiana
Jackson County

Before me the subscribing Justice within
the state and County aforesaid personally appeared Henry Vance and acknowledged the
execution of the above deed to be his voluntary act and deed for the purposes therein
mentioned. Given under my hand and seal this 20th day of April 1838.
John H. Thompson
Justice of the Peace

Recorded the 22nd day of October 1838 as examined by
L. S. Steele C.C. R.M.C.
