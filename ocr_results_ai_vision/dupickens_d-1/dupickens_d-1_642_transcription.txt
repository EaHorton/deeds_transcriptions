David Pugh

Know all men by these presents, that I David Pugh, of the state
of South Carolina, in the county of Fairfield, for and in consideration of the
natural love and affection which I have for James B. Wakefield
of the same state and county, have given and granted, and by these presents,
as you may grant unto the said James B. Wakefield his executors, administrators, and assigns
one negro boy named Dick, aged sixty years, one negro girl named Rachel, aged six years,
also, one mule, one yoke of oxen, one cow and calf, and one tract of land, containing
acres, with the possession of the above property, at my decease, and not before. To have and to
hold the said property above granted, unto the said James B. Wakefield, his executors,
administrators, and assigns forever. And I the said David Pugh, for myself, my heirs, executors,
and administrators, do covenant and agree to and with the said James B. Wakefield, his
executors, administrators, and assigns, to warrant and defend the title to the said property
against the claims of all persons whomsoever. In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand
and seal, this 5th day of June, in the year of our Lord one thousand eight hundred and forty
three.

Signed, sealed, and delivered in the presence of

David Pugh (Seal)

Solomon Ottley, Andrew Jenkins, Richard Conn
