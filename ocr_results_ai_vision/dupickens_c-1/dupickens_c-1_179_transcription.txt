To have and to hold all and singular the premises above mentioned unto the 
said John Cotton his heirs and assigns forever. And we do hereby bind ourselves and 
heirs executors and administrators to warrant and forever defend all and singular the said 
premises unto the said John Cotton his heirs and assigns against ourselves and our 
heirs and against every person or persons lawfully claiming or to claim the same 
or any part thereof. Witness our hands and seals this 2nd day of October in the year 1834.
Signed sealed and delivered in the presence of
Alexander Voyes, Benjamin Varnal

State of Carolina } This day before me personally appeared Alexander Voyes and 
Pickens District } made oath that he saw Charles Townsend, Daniel Fullerton, 
Jefferson Cotton, signing sealing and deliver the within deed or instrument of 
writing and conveyance to John Cotton for the use and purposes within mentioned 
and that he saw Benjamin Varnal sign the same as a witness with himself. 
Sworn to and subscribed before me this 11th day of March 1835.
Alex Voyes JP
Recorded the 11th day of March 1835 and examined by me

Charles Hewit
to
Charles Whitworth

State of Carolina } Know all men by these presents that I 
Pickens District } Charles Hewit of the state and district 
aforesaid for and in consideration of the natural love and affec- 
tion which I bear to Charles Whitworth of said district have 
given and granted and by these presents do give and grant unto the said Charles Whit- 
worth his executors administrators and assigns one negro child named Rees to 
age three weeks. To have and to hold the said negro child named Rees unto the 
said Charles Whitworth his executors administrators and assigns forever. And I the 
said Charles Hewit for this and myself my heirs executors and administrators do 
warrant and forever defend the said negro child named Rees unto the said Charles 
Whitworth his executors administrators and assigns against the claim of me the said Charles Hewit my executors and admin- 
istrators and against the claim of all and every other person or persons whatsoever. 
In testimony whereof I have hereunto set my hand and seal this 6th 
day of March 1835.
Charles Hewit (Seal)

State of Carolina } Personally appeared before me John Cotton and made oath that he

Andrew Hinton sign the same as a witness with himself sworn to and sub- 
scribed before me this 11th day of March 1835.
John Cotton
Alex Voyes JP
Recorded the 11th day of March 1835 and Examined by me
William L. Keith C.C. & M.E.
