State of South Carolina  I William L. Keith Clerk of the Court and Escheator
Pickens District  one of the Justices of the Quorum for the District aforesaid
Do hereby certify unto all whom it may concern, that Mary Head the wife of the
above mentioned Henry Head this day appeared before me and upon being by
me privately and separately examined as directed, that she does freely voluntarily
and without any compulsion dread or fear of any person or persons whomsoever
freely voluntarily renounce release and forever relinquish unto the before men-
tioned John Harrison all her interest and estate and also all her right and
Estate, and also all her right, and Claim of Dower, of in or to all and singular the
premises within and before mentioned and released
Mary x Head
her mark
Given under my hand and seal this Eleventh day of January in the year
of our Lord one thousand eight hundred and thirty seven
William L. Keith (Seal)
C.C. & Esff. D.C.

South Carolina  Personally appeared John C. Grisham and made
Pickens District  oath that he was present and did see Henry Head sign seal
and deliver the within deed of Conveyance for the uses and purposes within ex-
pressed, and that he did also see L. F. Stokes with himself witness the
due execution of the same in presence of each other sworn to this 11th day of
January 1837
John C. Grisham
Before me W. L. Keith C.C.
Recorded the 11th day of January 1837 and Examined by me
W. L. Keith C.C. & H.M.C.

John Harrison
To
Celia Robinson

State of South Carolina  To all people to whom these presents shall
Pickens District  come I John Harrison do send greeting
Know ye that the said John Harrison of the state and District
aforesaid, for and in consideration of the love good will and
affection which I have and bear towards my loving daughter Selah Robinson of the
said State and District have given and granted and by these presents do give and grant
unto the said Selah Robinson her heirs Executors and administrators one negro girl
named Rhody out of my Estate freely forever and the said Selah Robinson her heirs
and assigns to hold possess and enjoy the said girl Rhody as their own proper
right and property before the signing and delivery of the said girl Rhody have and
to the said Selah Robinson her heirs Executors and Administrators
from henceforth and forever with absolutely without any manner of condition
In witness whereof I have hereunto set my hand and seal this twenty fifth day of November in the year of
our Lord one thousand eight hundred and thirty five
Test. John Stokes
John Harrison

South Carolina  This day personally appeared John Adair before me and made
Pickens District  oath that he was present and saw the within deed signed
sealed delivered for the within named purposes 16th January 1837
Before me John Burside J.P.  John Adair
