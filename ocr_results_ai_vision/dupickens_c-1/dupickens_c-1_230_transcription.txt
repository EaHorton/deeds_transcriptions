To
Robert Wilson

The State of South Carolina
This Indenture made this sixth day of September in the year of our Lord one thousand eight hundred and thirty six between William Wilson, Esquire, Sheriff of Anderson District in the State aforesaid of the one part and Robert Wilson of the said State and District of the other part. Whereas the said William Wilson, Esquire, Sheriff as aforesaid by virtue of a writ of fieri facias out of the Court of Common Pleas at Anderson Court House to him directed at the suit of James Green against the goods and chattels, lands and tenements of William Wilson, deceased, did seize and take in execution all that plantation or tract of land above mentioned as to him directed, after many proclamations thereon made according to the law did expose the same to public sale at Anderson Court House on the first Monday in January last, and Robert Wilson, being the highest bidder, for the sum of one thousand six hundred and thirty five dollars, did purchase the same. Now this Indenture witnesseth that the said William Wilson, Esquire, Sheriff as aforesaid in consideration of the sum of one thousand six hundred and thirty five dollars to him in hand paid by the said Robert Wilson at and before the sealing and delivery of these presents the receipt whereof is hereby acknowledged, hath granted, bargained, sold and released and by these presents doth grant, bargain, sell and release unto the said Robert Wilson, his heirs and assigns forever, all that plantation or tract of land containing one hundred and fifty acres more or less situate in the District and State aforesaid on the waters of Saluda River, bounded by lands of James Green, Robert Wilson, Early Harrison, etc. together with all and singular the rights, members, hereditaments and appurtenances to the said premises belonging or in anywise incident or appertaining. To have and to hold all and singular the premises before mentioned unto the said Robert Wilson, his heirs and assigns forever. And the said William Wilson, Esquire, Sheriff as aforesaid doth hereby covenant and agree to and with the said Robert Wilson, his heirs and assigns that he the said William Wilson, Esquire, Sheriff as aforesaid hath not done or committed any act, matter or thing whatsoever whereby the premises hereby granted or any part thereof is or shall be impeached, charged or incumbered in title, charge, estate or otherwise howsoever. In witness whereof the said William Wilson, Esquire, Sheriff as aforesaid hath hereunto set his hand and seal the day and year first above written.

Sealed and delivered in the presence of
W. Leake
G. F. Patton

Recorded the 15th day of September 1836
