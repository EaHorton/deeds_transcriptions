Henry Garner
To
James Garner

South Carolina
Pickens District

201

Received of James Garner six hundred
and fifty dollars in full payment for a
negro man named Stephen about twenty two years old which
I warrant the right and title to the said James Garner
him his heirs and assigns forever against me my heirs or any person claiming
the said warrant from me and a warranty and a slave for life as witness
my hand and seal this 1st February 1836.
Henry Garner

I acknowledge the within title of sale to be my act and deed and the legal rights
of the said negro to James Garner this 1st day of August 1836
In presence of L. Keitch C.C.
Henry Garner
Recorded the 1st day of August 1836 and examined by me
W. L. Keith C.C. & H. M. C.

Sheriff Haynes
To
John Clayton

State of South Carolina, Know all men by these presents that I
Robert Quidell Sheriff of Pickens District in the said District
aforesaid for and in consideration of the sum of two hundred
dollars to me in hand paid by John Clayton of the said District
have bargained and sold and by these presents do bargain sell and
release unto the said John Clayton all that tract or parcel of land lying and
being in the District aforesaid on the East side of Twelve Mile River and adjoining lands of B. Duncan
and others beginning at a stake corner near a stake corner thence S 40 E 62.5 chains and 20 links
to a post oak corner thence S 85 E 39.5 chains and 20 links to a small red oak thence
N 10 W 45 chains 20 links to a linn on the bank of said River thence down the said River
according to the meanders of the same to the beginning corner which is a hickory on the East bank of
the district River chain containing eighty two acres of the same more or less. Together with
all and singular the rights members hereditaments and appurtenances to the said premises
belonging To Have And To Hold all and singular the said premises unto him the said
John Clayton his heirs and assigns Executed an Administrators forever. And the hereby
grantor myself my heirs executors and administrators do warrant and forever defend
all and singular the said premises, with the said John Clayton his assigns executors
administrators and every other person or persons whomsoever lawfully claiming
or to claim the same or any part of the same. In witness whereof I have hereunto
set my hand and seal this 1st day of August in the year of our Lord one thousand
eight hundred and thirty six and in the sixtieth year of American Independence
Signed sealed and delivered in the presence of
A. M. Patton, Test. William A. Patton
Robert Quidell
his
Henry X Haynes
mark

The State of South Carolina on this day personally came and appeared before me James McDoon
Pickens District
A. M. Patton and made oath in due form of law and sayeth on oath that he
saw Sheriff Haynes sign seal and deliver the within sale to John Clayton as his act
and deed and that he subscribed before me
James McDoon
Recorded the 18th day of August 1836 and examined by W. L. Keith C.C. & H. M. C.
