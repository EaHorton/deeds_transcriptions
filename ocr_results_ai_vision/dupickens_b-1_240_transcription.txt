The State of South Carolina	Office of the Registry In Chancery, Petition for
Charleston District	Partition Filed 21 April 1832 Bivens & Smith, Sol. for Petitioners.

Exparte
Patrick Noble Trustee of J. Howard, Clk & Register in Equity for the Judicial
District aforesaid, do hereby certify that the annexed schedules
of negro slaves marked A is the portion or share of Eliza A. Pickens the wife of
Thomas B. Pickens, allotted to her out of which a block by the Court of Equity for
the said District upon the division and repartition in the said petition by the
Commissioners appointed by the Court under and for execution of the Decree of Partition
filed in this case. The Commissioners report was confirmed by the Court in this
Office on the 21st day of April 1832 and confirmed on the 11th day of May 1832.
Eliza A. Pickens is entitled to one equal share, one of which said division
and partition was made by the said Commissioners. Certified the annexed
schedules of the said share written by this Office is duly endorsed by the Register
in the Marriage Settlement.	Registered J. S. Thomas

Thomas J. Gantt, Register in Equity & Clerk Register
Aforesaid have hereunto set my hand and the seal
of the said Court of Equity at Charleston this Eleventh
day of May in the year of our Lord one thousand
eight hundred and thirty two.

[Seal of Office]

Thomas J. Gantt
R.E. & C.
