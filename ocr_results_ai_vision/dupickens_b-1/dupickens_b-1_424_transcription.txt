George W. Keith
To
Mary Sutherland & others

The State of South Carolina
Pickens District

To Thomas J. Sloane & Washington
Sloan of the District and State
aforesaid formerly of the State of Georgia Lumpkin County
Know ye that I George W. Keith of the said state & County for and
in consideration of the sum of Two Thousand dollars to Mary Sutherland & others
of South Carolina that I will make or cause to be made a good and sufficient right and
title to the interest in the plantation or piece of land known and distinguished as the
formerly known and now free adjoining known there is a quit title to Mary Sutherland
& Thomas Child now being the Estate of Thomas Sutherland Dec’d. And for the security
of the performance of the above obligation I do hereby assign and transfer all my
spare or unappropriated claims to the said Mary Sutherland & others from the title
therein derived from the aforesaid Thomas Sutherland Dec’d. The proper quit claim deed
from the said Grantor to come in and be made by the said Mary Sutherland that
is attached to me being my wife’s interest in said Estate I do have and to hold and
dispose of the said spare claims to the said Mary Sutherland & others forever and
to their heirs and assigns forever and I do covenant and agree to and with the said
Mary Sutherland & others that I am now seized of a good and indefeasible estate of
inheritance in fee simple of and in the said premises and have good right and
lawful authority to sell and convey the same in manner and form aforesaid and
that the said premises now are and forever after shall remain free and clear of all
incumbrances whatsoever lawfully claiming or to claim the same or any part thereof
Provided always nevertheless and it is hereby agreed by and
between the said parties that if the said George W. Keith shall make or cause to be made
unto by the twenty fifth day of December in the year of our Lord one thousand eight
hundred and thirty five a good and sufficient right and title to a certain plantation or
a tract of land known Thomas Sutherland and formerly known and a small field
adjoining known there is a Good title to the said Thomas Sutherland & Thomas Child
the said George W. Keith his heirs executors and administrators to him in such case of
agreement in the assignment of the Estate of the said Thomas J. Sutherland Dec’d and
the said George W. Keith further agrees to deliver the said four negroes to the said
Mary Sutherland & others for them to remain in the possession until the title is made
which will be on the 25th Dec 1835 or until the said title is perfected according to
law and should the said G. W. Keith attempt to take the said property out of possession
of the said Mary Sutherland & others before the said time, it is agreed in
state of the title, and in case of any interference it may be proper and correct to
proceed to the same in a proper manner and the effect of the above lien for the title
but should the proper claims come in possession of the said Mary Sutherland & others
heirs or assigns until the said title is made according to law then this agreement
and every clause and article therein contained shall cease and be null and void
null and void it all intents and purposes Otherwise to continue in full force and
virtue in law In testimony whereof I have hereunto set my hand and seal this
twenty second day of October in the year of our Lord one thousand eight hundred
and thirty five and in the fifty eighth year of American Independence
Signed sealed and delivered in the presence of
J. W. Keith
