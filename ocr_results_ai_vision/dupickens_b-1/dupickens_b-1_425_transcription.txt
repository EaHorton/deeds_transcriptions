Signed Sealed and acknowledged in the presence of
W. L. Keith
Test Robert Holsey, John Louthan

State of Carolina Personally appeared Robert Holsey an man oath that he saw
Richmond District George McKeith sign seal and deliver the within Mortgage for the
uses purposes within mentioned and that he with John Louthan did sign the same as
a subscribing witness to the same sworn to the 3rd day of Jany 1831
Before Wm. L. Keith C.C. Robert Holsey
Recorded the 3rd day of February 1831 and Examined by
William L. Keith C.C. & R.M.C.

George McKeith
to
Mary Louthan and others

The State of South Carolina
Richmond District

Know all men by these presents that
I George Washington McKeith of the
District and state aforesaid (formerly of the State of Virginia) am held and firmly bound unto the State of South Carolina and unto Robert Louthan his
Certain Attorney in fact administrators assigns in the penal sum of five thousand
Dollars to which payment well and truly to be made. I bind myself my heirs Executors and
Administrators firmly by these presents sealed with my seal and dated this twenty
day of December in the year of our Lord one thousand eight hundred and thirty
The Condition of the above obligation is such that the above bound Negro Washington
McKeith his heirs Executors Administrators or assigns shall well and truly pay or
cause by the twenty fifth day of December in the year of our Lord one thousand eight
hundred and thirty five one and sufficient unto and title to his interest to the Louthan
plantation free of charges taxes or incumbrances whatsoever and that the said
Louthan being the plantation whereon Ann Louthan deceased formerly lived and
whereon was at this day before the first day of May of his part of said Estate and
to take any agreement between the heirs of said Estate
