said Francis Brock his executors, administrators, attor-
nies, or agent from time to time and at all times hereafter
peaceably and quietly to enter into and upon the messuage,
or tenement, of the said Willis Robinson in the possession of
the said slaves Rix, Maria & Mary & her child into his custody,
and possession, and them to take and detain to his
own use, and behalf as his own proper goods, chattels, from
thenceforth and forever, in the same case and as if upon
the same pleas, returning the overplus (if any there
happen to be) after paying the said sum of Twelve
hundred and Thirty nine 95/100 dollars with interest
the same unto the said Francis Brock his executors
administrators or assigns. In witness whereof the said
Willis Robinson has hereunto set my hand and seal this
